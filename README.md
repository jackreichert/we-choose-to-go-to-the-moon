# We Choose to Go to the Moon

**[wechoosetogotothemoon.com](https://wechoosetogotothemoon.com/)**

A tribute to JFK's 1962 Rice University speech and humanity's journey to the Moon — past, present, and future.

Covers the full text and video of Kennedy's address, a chronological timeline of every major lunar mission from 1959 to today, and the missions still to come — including Artemis, Chang'e, and the new era of commercial lunar exploration.

Pure static HTML/CSS/JS. No framework, no build step.
